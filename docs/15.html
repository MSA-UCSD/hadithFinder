IAW 2024
مَنْ أَحَبَّ لِلَّهِ وَأَبْغَضَ لِلَّهِ وَأَعْطَى لِلَّهِ وَمَنَعَ لِلَّهِ فَقَدِ اسْتَكْمَلَ الإِيمَانَ
Whoever loves for Allah's sake and hates for Allah's sake, gives for Allah's sake and withholds for Allah's sake, has perfected his faith.
(Abu Dawud)
